Why "posts liked" ≫ "posts viewed": Instagram's export logs every like you've ever made (a complete history), but only includes a small recent window of "posts & videos viewed" — not your true view count. So liked (complete) far exceeds viewed (partial snapshot); you obviously viewed far more than the export reveals.
